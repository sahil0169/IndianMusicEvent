About the Festival

The Indian Classical Music & Dance Festival is an annual event celebrating the rich heritage of Indian classical arts. The festival features performances by renowned artists in the fields of music and dance, offering a vibrant platform to showcase the cultural traditions of India. This edition is dedicated to Rashid Khan and Ganpati Bhatt.

Dates: August 15, 2024

Venue: Jio World Plaza, Mumbai

Pandit Kumar Gandharva

Pandit Kumar Gandharva was a renowned Hindustani classical singer known for his distinctive singing style and innovative approach to music. He learned from BR Deodhar, who influenced his voice culture significantly.

Baldev Singh

Baldev Singh Bali is an Indian classical musician and the Professor Emeritus at the Institute of Music and Fine Arts in Jammu. He has also served as the Principal of this institution and studied at Jammu & Kashmir University.

Ganpati Bhatt

Ganapati Bhat, also known as Ganapati Bhat Hasanagi, is an Indian Hindustani classical vocalist associated with the Kirana-Gwalior Gharana. He is recognized for his contributions to the genre of classical music
